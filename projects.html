Analysis and Simulation of Cache Replacement algorithms

This project was done as a part of course ES215 : Computer Organization and Architecture at IIT Gandhinagar.

Team members:

  • Abhinav Meena (abhinav.meena@iitgn.ac.in)
  • Priyam Tongia (priyam.tongia@iitgn.ac.in)

When the cache is full and a miss is inquired, it must be decided which cache block to evict. In this project, we wrote the code, analysed and simulated some replacement policies on a few traces using the ChampSim simulator. The policies that we considered are : LRU, MRU, FIFO, Bimodal-Insertion , NRU , SRRIP and DRRIP.
We observed that any single policy cannot give optimal performance on all traces and hence methods like Set-Dueling must be employed to dynamically choose a policy during runtime.
